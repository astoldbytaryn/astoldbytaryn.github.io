Here's a list of my favorite and most played games!

Overwatch 2

  • Overwatch 2 is a multiplayer 5 versus 5 Hero Shooter.
  • There are 3 types of character types you can play as: Tank, DPS and Support.
  • I main support but I can play DPS when necessary. I am also learning how to play Tank.
  • Overwatch is available to play on console and PC.

Splatoon 3

  • Splatoon 3 is a multiplayer 4 versus 4 action shooter only available on Nintendo Switch.
  • You play as an inkling and your goal is to quickly cover the map in your teams color ink.
  • Fun fact: Splatoon 2 is the game that I started to gain traction from when I first started streaming.

Sims 4

  • Sims 4 is a life simulation game that gives players the ability to controls the lives of people (Sims).
  • Sims is actually a game that I play in my spare time. You can't play it online but mods make the game very fun and add a more realistic feel to the game.
  • I actually started getting heavy into Sims at the end of production for Sims 3. It can be very addicting to play this game!
